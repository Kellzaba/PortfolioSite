Design Dilemma

The client needed two newsletters to be laid out. Usually, their Executive Summary newsletter has around 8-12 articles, but this edition had a record-setting 25! This edition of their Privacy in Focus newsletter had a much more reasonable total of 3 articles, but additionally needed a custom ad designed.

Reasoning & Process

Wiley Rein’s newsletters have a general “template” for their appearance, so I stuck to their layout standards and visual branding. The first step was to get all of the text into the InDesign document and properly formatted to get an idea of how much space each story would take up on a page. Then, I laid out all of the stories in the correct order and taking up the smallest amount of space possible (within the layout framework.) Next, I checked the page count to make sure it was valid to be printed as a booklet. Commence the arduous process of shuffling, tweaking, and fixing things like rags, widows, and orphans until every element is in its proper place.

Solution

The Executive Summary newsletter was neatly laid out over 22 pages and the Privacy in Focus was laid out over 6 pages, both containing a single-page insert. Both editions will be posted to Wiley Rein’s website and printed out as physical booklets to be distributed among their members.
