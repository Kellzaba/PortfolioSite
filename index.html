Hi! I'm Kelly Pavao.

Let me help you succeed.

Some designers are enamored with pixels; some idolize typography; some are devoted to color. So what makes me different? I have a passion for helping other people succeed at what they do. You love what you do, and I want to help you, through design, to do it better.

Organize All the Things

One of my superpowers is being able to take disorganized piles of things and sort through and group them into neat, efficient collections. I'm both a big-picture person and a detail-oriented one: let me lay out all the elements, and then I can knowingly dive into the nitty-gritty.

Imagining Creative Solutions

I love puzzles. Give me a set of rules that all need to be followed, and I relish the challenge of being able to satisfy every requirement. I believe that restrictions promote creativity; learn to think outside of the box to fit everything into the box.
